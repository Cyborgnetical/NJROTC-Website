11 Navy General Orders
  1. To take charge of this post and all government property in view.
  2. To walk my post in a military manner, keeping always on the alert and obversing everything that takes place within sight or hearing.
  3. To report all violations of orders I am instructed to enforce.
  4. To reapet all calls more distant from the guard house than my own.
  5. To quit my post only when properly relived
  6. To recieve obey and pass on the sentry who relives me, all orders from the commanding officer, command duty officer, officer of the deck, and officers, and petty officers of the watch only.
  7. To talk to no one except in the line of duty.
  8. To give the alarm in case of fire or disorder.
  9. To call the officer of the deck in any case not covered by instruction.
  10. To salute all officer and colors and standards not cased.
  11. To call the officer of the deck in any case not covered by instruction
  12. To be especially watchfull at night and during times of challenging to challenge all people on or near my post, and to allow no one to pass without proper athority
